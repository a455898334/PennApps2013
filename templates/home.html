amiGo


amiGo is an application that allows strangers who are at the same event to trade contact information. Whether it is for organizing the next house party or making a class study group on Facebook, amiGo will allow everyone in the vicinity to trade Facebook pages and keep in touch after the event is over. Plus, amiGo is a self-contained web application, so there is no need to even ask everyone to download an application.
This application is brought to you by Curtis Bezault, Daryl Sew, Shan Shi, and Kevin Wang
